# Directive: Project Overview

## What Is This

The Kapruka Agent Challenge 2026 is a competition for Sri Lankan developers to build the most innovative AI shopping agent on the Kapruka MCP server. The best entry wins an Apple M4 Mac Mini (USD 799).

We are building a hosted, full-screen chat shopping experience that connects to the live Kapruka platform and lets customers browse, discover, and checkout.

## About Kapruka

- Sri Lanka's largest online shopping and gift delivery platform
- Publicly listed on the Colombo Stock Exchange
- Trusted by 1.2 million Sri Lankan expatriates worldwide
- Offices in Sri Lanka, USA, UK, and Australia
- Categories: cakes, flowers, chocolates, clothing, electronics, grocery, hampers, jewelry, cosmetics, personalized gifts, and much more
- Heavy gifting focus — people send gifts from abroad to family in Sri Lanka
- Also used for everyday personal shopping

## The MCP Server

- Endpoint: `https://mcp.kapruka.com/mcp`
- Free and public — no API key needed
- Capabilities:
  - Search products
  - Browse categories
  - Quote delivery to any Sri Lankan address
  - Create guest-checkout orders with pay links
  - Track existing orders
- Full documentation: https://mcp.kapruka.com

## The Judging Rubric (out of 100)

| Criteria | Points |
|---|---|
| Experience & polish | 30 |
| Visual richness | 20 |
| Personality | 15 |
| Usefulness | 15 |
| End-to-end completeness | 15 |
| Creativity | 5 |

**Bonus points for:**
- Multi-item carts
- Delivery date constraints
- Gift messaging
- Tanglish conversation
- Sinhala language support

## Submission Requirements

- Live, public URL that works when judges open it
- Full-screen chat UI
- Must go all the way from discovery to checkout
- Deadline: 30 June 2026

## Target Audience

Two main user types:
1. Sri Lankan expats abroad sending gifts home to family
2. People in Sri Lanka shopping for themselves or gifting locally

Both groups benefit from a conversational agent that helps them navigate Kapruka's massive catalog and get to checkout quickly.

## What Makes This Agent Win

The judging is 50 points on experience, polish, and visual richness combined. The agent that feels the best wins — not necessarily the most technically complex one. Our strategy is to build something that feels alive, looks beautiful, and genuinely helps people shop — with smart gifting intelligence built in under the hood.
